# Coffee Expansion SQL Project

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze the online sales data of **Monday Coffee**, a company selling coffee products since January 2023, and to recommend the **top three Indian cities** for opening new physical coffee shop locations based on **consumer demand, sales performance, and cost feasibility**.

---

## Key Business Questions

### 1. Coffee Consumer Estimation
How many people in each city are estimated to consume coffee, assuming **25% of the population** drinks coffee?

### 2. Total Revenue (Q4 2023)
What is the total revenue generated from coffee sales across all cities in the **last quarter of 2023**?

### 3. Product Sales Volume
How many units of each coffee product have been sold?

### 4. Average Sales per Customer (City-wise)
What is the average sales amount per customer in each city?

### 5. City Population vs Coffee Consumers
For each city, what is the population and the estimated number of coffee consumers?

### 6. Top-Selling Products by City
What are the **top 3 selling coffee products** in each city based on sales volume?

### 7. Customer Segmentation by City
How many **unique customers** have purchased coffee products in each city?

### 8. Average Sale vs Rent Analysis
What is the **average sales per customer** and **average rent per customer** for each city?

### 9. Monthly Sales Growth
What is the **month-over-month sales growth or decline** trend?

### 10. Market Potential Analysis
Identify the **top 3 cities** based on:
- total sales  
- total customers  
- estimated coffee consumers  
- rent feasibility  

---

## Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following cities are recommended for opening new coffee shop locations:

### 1. Pune
- Highest total revenue among all cities  
- Average sales per customer is high  
- Average rent per customer is relatively low, improving profitability  

### 2. Delhi
- Highest estimated coffee-consuming population (~7.7 million)  
- Strong customer base  
- Average rent per customer (~₹330) remains within a reasonable range  

### 3. Jaipur
- One of the highest numbers of unique customers  
- Very low average rent per customer (~₹156)  
- Strong average sales per customer (~₹11.6k), indicating good spending behavior  

---

## Conclusion
By combining **market size**, **actual sales performance**, **customer behavior**, and **cost considerations**, **Pune, Delhi, and Jaipur** emerge as the most suitable cities for **Monday Coffee’s physical store expansion**.
